Overview

RV College of Engineering (RVCE) is a premier technical institution in India, established in 1963 and managed by the non-profit Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST). It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. In the year of 2023 total 214 recruiters visited the campus and 910 0ffers were made by them as of now, highest offer made by the recruiters is 38 Lakh(2021) and the average package of RVCE is 7 LPA
